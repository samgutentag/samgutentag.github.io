Garlic Aioli

Formatted

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Servings: 1 cup Source: spendwithpennies.com

Ingredients

½ cup mayonnaise

4 clove garlic, crushed (to paste with mortar and pestle ideally)

1 Tbsp lemon juice

¼ tsp kosher salt

2 tsp olive oil

¼ tsp pepper

Directions

Combine all ingredients together and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

Store in airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 10 days.
