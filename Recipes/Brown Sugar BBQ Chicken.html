Brown Sugar BBQ Chicken

Formatted

Prep Time: 0 Cook Time: 4 Servings: 6 servings Source: delish.com

Ingredients

2 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts

1 cup barbecue sauce, plus more for drizzling

1/2 cup brown sugar

1/4 cup bourbon

1/4 cup Italian dressing

2 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp paprika

Kosher salt

Freshly ground black pepper

6 Potato buns

Coleslaw, for serving

Directions

In a slow-cooker, add chicken breasts, barbecue sauce, brown sugar, bourbon, Italian dressing, garlic powder and paprika. Season with salt and pepper.

Toss until well coated, then cover and cook on high for 4 hours or on low for 6 hours.

Shred chicken and serve on buns with a drizzle of barbecue sauce and a spoonful of coleslaw.
