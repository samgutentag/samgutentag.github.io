Herb Pan Sauce

Formatted

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 Source: Joy of Cooking, page 546

Ingredients

1 shallot, minced

2/3 cup dry white wine

1 Tbsp lemon juice

4 tsp Dijon Mustard

1 bay leaf

1/2 cup heavy cream

1 1/2 tsp butter

2 Tbsp parsley, tarragon, thyme, or combination, minced

Directions

After pan frying meats, transfer them to a platter to keep warm.

Pour off most of excess fat and set pan over medium heat and add shallots.

Cook, stirring until softened but not browned.

Increase heat to medium high and add wine, cider, or stock.

Stir with a wooden spoon to loosen and dissolve any browned bits from pan.

Bring to boil and add lemon juice, mustard and bay leaf.

Cook, stirring occasionally until slightly reduced and thickened, about 2 minutes.

Add heavy cream, cook until reduced and thickened, about 2 minutes.

Remove from heat and stir in butter in small bits to not break sauce.

Stir in minced herbs and serve.
