Strombolli

Formatted

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 30 min Servings: 4 Source: Mark Gutentag

Ingredients

1 Frozen Bread dough OR Pillsburry Pizza Dough

2 eggs

2 Tbsp Italian Seasoning

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

1 pkg sliced pepperoni

1 pkg sliced salami

Directions

Preheat oven to 425 degrees

Spread dough into a rectangle, patch holes with pieces from corners

Beat an egg and schmear over the dough.

Sprinkle Italian seasoning onto dough, over egg schmear

Lay down a layer of fillings in a straight line, shingles on the middle 1/3 of the dough

Fold over one side and repeat layer of filling, fold over last edge and pinch ends closed.

Transfer to cookie sheet and slice small vent holes along the length of the dough, about 1/2 inch long, every 4 inches or so

Bake for 18-20 minutes or until browned on top.

Allow to cool about 10 minutes before slicing into 1 inch strips.

Serve with Pizza Sauce to great effect!
