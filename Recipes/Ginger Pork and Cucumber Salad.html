Ginger Pork and Cucumber Salad

Formatted

Prep Time: 15 Cook Time: 0 hours Servings: 4 Source: goodhousekeeping.com

Ingredients

1 cup long-grain white rice


1 lb ground pork

1 Tbsp canola oil


2 garlic cloves, finely chopped

1 small red chili, finely chopped

2 Tbsp ginger, grated


2 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce

2 limes, juiced


1 tsp brown sugar

1 English cucumber, thinly sliced

2 scallions, thinly sliced

1 bunch fresh cilantro, diced

1/2 bunch fresh mint, diced

Directions

Brown ground pork in cast-iron skillet in canola oil, 7 minutes.

Toss with garlic, red chile, and grated fresh ginger.

Remove from heat and toss with half lime juice and half low-sodium soy sauce (adding 1/4 cup water if dry).

Whisk remaining lime juice, low-sodium soy sauce, and brown sugar.

Toss with cucumber and scallions; fold with cilantro and mint.

Serve with pork over rice.

Nutrition

(per serving): About 475 calories, 22.5 g fat (6 g saturated), 26 g protein, 435 mg sodium, 44 g carb, 3 g fiber
