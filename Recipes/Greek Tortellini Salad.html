Greek Tortellini Salad

Formatted

Prep Time: 20 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6 Source: delish.com

Ingredients

27oz refrigerated cheese tortellini


2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 cucumber, cut into half moons

1/2 red onion, diced

1 cup kalamata olives, halved lengthwise

1 1/2 cup crumbled feta


Dressing:

1/3 cup freshly chopped dill

2/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil

1/3 cup red wine vinegar

2 garlic cloves, minced

2 tsp dried oregano

2 tsp dried thyme

Kosher salt

Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

Cook tortellini according to pasta instructions. Drain.

Meanwhile, in a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, mustard, and season generously with salt and pepper.

Add tomatoes, onion, cucumber, olives, and dill and stir until combined, then add cooked tortellini and stir until coated.
