Kali’s Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Source: Kali’s Kitchen @ Clif Bar HQ

Ingredients

2½ sticks butter, room temperature

282g brown sugar

227g white sugar

2 eggs, room temp

2 tsp vanilla

482g all purpose flour

1¼ tsp baking soda

1½ tsp baking powder

1½ tsp kosher salt

330g dark chocolate wafers, Guittard 66%

Directions

Cream together at high speed butter, brown sugar, white sugar

For more information on creaming, see this link

Once creamed, slow mixer to low speed and add vanilla and the first egg.

Wait for each egg to be incorporated before adding the next until all are combined.

In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and kosher salt.

Add dry mixture to the butter egg mixture. Mix on low until just combined .

You should see a hint of dry flour in the bowl, then add dark chocolate wafers.

Mix until just barely combined DO NOT OVER MIX

Scoop these onto a baking sheet, roughly 100g or ⅓ cup each.

Cover with plastic wrap and chill, can be baked straight away but better with age.

Preheat oven to 340°F, line baking sheet with parchment paper

Place well chilled cookies about 2 inches apart on the baking sheet.

Bake in lower middle oven rack for about 12-16 minutes rotating halfway through.

As soon as they come out of the oven give them a sprinkle of course salt.

Let them chill out on the the sheet pan before eating.

Enjoy
