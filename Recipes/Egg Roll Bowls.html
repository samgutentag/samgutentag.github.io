Egg Roll Bowls

Formatted

Prep Time: 0 Cook Time: 0 Servings: 6 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

1 Tbsp vegetable oil

4 eggs

1 garlic cloves, minced

1 Tbsp ginger, minced

1 lb ground pork

1 Tbsp sesame oil

1/2 onion, thinly sliced

2 carrots, shredded

1/4 green cabbage, thinly sliced

1/4 cup soy sauce

1 Tbsp Sriracha

1 bunch green onion, thinly sliced

1 Tbsp sesame seeds

2 cups white rice

Directions

In a large wok (or skillet) over medium heat, heat vegetable oil.

Add garlic and ginger and cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.

Add pork and cook until no pink remains.

Meanwhile start rice.

Push pork to the side and add sesame oil.

Add onion, carrot, and cabbage.

Stir to combine with meat and add soy sauce and Sriracha.

Cook until cabbage is tender, 5 to 8 minutes.

Make a small hole at the bottom of the pot and crack in eggs, whisk to slightly scramble and then stir to coat the other ingredients.

Transfer mixture to a serving dish and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.

Serve.
