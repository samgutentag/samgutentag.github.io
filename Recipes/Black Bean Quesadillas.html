Black Bean Quesadillas

Formatted

Cook Time: 1 hour Servings: 6 Source: mobkitchen.co.uk

Ingredients

3 Large Avocados

3 Bell Peppers, diced

1 white onion, diced

2 15 oz cans black beans, drained

4 tsp smoked paprika

1 15oz can whole corn, drained

3 cups shredded cheddar cheese

9 Large Tortilla Wraps

Directions

Black Bean Fillings

Place onions and peppers in a frying pan on a medium heat.

Once softened, add smoked paprika. Allow to soften further for another 5 minutes.

Wrap two corn on the cob in tin foil.

Add your black beans to the pan.

Add salt, pepper and a handful of chopped coriander.

Mash everything together and remove from heat.

Quesadillas

Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium high heat.

Prep tortillas by placing a layer of cheese, then been onion mixture, then corn, then more cheese onto half of the tortilla.

Fold over into a half circle, place in pan and gently press to seal outside edge.

Cook for 3-4 minutes, and then flip it.

The wraps should be golden, and the cheese should be melted.

Remove from the heat and slice into thirds.

Notes

This always works best when made in half circles, a single tortilla folded over itself to enclose your fillings.
