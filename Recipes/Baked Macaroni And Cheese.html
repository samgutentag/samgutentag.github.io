Baked Macaroni And Cheese

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 30 Total Time: 40 Servings: 8 Source: Joy of Cooking, page 296

Ingredients

2 cup Bechamel White Sauce

1/2 medium onion, minced

1 bay leaf

1/4 tsp. sweet paprika

2 cup grated sharp cheddar or Colby (8 oz)

2 cup elbow macaroni, small shells, or tubetti (8 oz)

1/2 cup buttered bread crumbs

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 F. Grease 8- or 9- inch square baking dish.

Put sharp cheddar or Colby cheese in freezer, makes grating easier.

Meanwhile, prepare double batch of White Sauce

Grate sharp cheddar or Colby cheese, set aside

Stir minced onion, bay leaf, and sweet paprika into White Sauce

Simmer gently, stirring often for 15 minutes

Meanwhile in large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions, al dente

Drain the pasta and return to hot pot, stir in white sauce until covered and well combined

Transfer to baking dish and sprinkle with remaining cheese.

Cover over the top with buttered bread crumbs.

Bake until breadcrumbs are lightly browned, about 30 minutes

Let stand 5 minutes before serving
