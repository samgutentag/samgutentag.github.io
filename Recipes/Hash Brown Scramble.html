Hash Brown Scramble

Prep Time: 5 Cook Time: 15 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2

Ingredients

1 Tbsp olive oil

1/2 bag hash browns

4 large eggs

3 cloves garlic, crushed or minced

1/2 large white onion, diced

1/2 lb hot pork breakfast sausage

1 green bell pepper, diced

1 cup shredded cheese

Directions

Heat oil in large pan. Add breakfast sausage to begin browning.

Let rest on one side until l crispy, then add crushed garlic and flip to cook through.

Remove from pan and set aside. Drain excess grease.

Drizzle pan with more oil and spread out frozen hash browns in even layer. Allow to cook until set, about 6 minutes.

Top with peppers and onions. Drizzle with oil and flip hash brown to cook other side. About 5 minutes.

Whisk eggs together in a medium bowl, then pour into a medium skillet over high heat.

Scramble eggs and sprinkle on cheese as they cook if desired.

Combine and serve.
