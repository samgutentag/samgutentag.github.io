Vitantonio Pecan Waffles

Formatted

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 15 min Servings: 6 waffles Source: chicagotribune.com

Ingredients

1/2 cup pecans, lightly toasted

1 1/3 cups flour

3 Tbsp sugar

1 Tbsp baking powder

1 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp salt

2 eggs

3 Tbsp melted butter

1 1/2 cups soda water or seltzer

Directions

Process pecans in food processor until fine

Add pecans, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt to large bowl, whisk to combine

Whisk eggs in medium bowl. Add butter and whisk 30 seconds.

Stir egg mixture and club soda into pecan mixture. Do not overmix!

Heat waffle iron until hot.

Brush with vegetable shortening.

Pour in about 1/2 cup batter.

Cook according to manual instructions.

Remove with wooden spatula when done. Repeat with remaining batter.

Keep warm in 275-degree oven before serving.
