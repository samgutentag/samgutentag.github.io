Babish Deep Dish Pizza

Servings: 12" Cast iron skillet, 4 servings Source: Binging with Babish

Ingredients

8.25 oz room temp water

2.25 tsp (1packet) active dry yeast

1 tsp sugar

355g All purpose Flour

70g Medium grind corn meal

1½ tsp kosher salt

⅛ tsp Cream of Tartar

½ cup vegetable oil


28 oz San Marzano Tomatos, Hand Crushed

1/2 white onion, finely diced

4 cloves garlic, crushed

2 Tbsp olive oil

2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes

2 tsp dried Oregano

2 tsp dried Basil

3 Tbsp Tomato paste


1 pound sausage, removed from casing

1 pkg sliced pepperoni

1 pkg sliced provolone

1 pkg sliced low moisture mozzarella

Directions

Pizza Crust

In a Stand Mixer Bowl combine water, dry active yeast and sugar. Allow to bloom for 10 minutes, should be foamy.

In another bowl which to combine flour, corn meal, kosher salt and cream of tartar.

Pour mixture into stand mixer bowl and add olive oil

Stir with dough hook on Low for 1-2 minutes, until dough comes from bowl

Stir on medium another 7-8 minutes to knead

Knead 1-2 minutes on floured surface

Stretch into ball, place into lightly oiled bowl, cover and let rest 60-90 minutes or until doubled in size*

Oil cast iron skillet and stretch dough into crust

Let rest, covered 25 minutes to relax

Pizza Sauce

In large sauce pan add olive oil and heat over medium heat until shimmering.

Add onion and sweat 2-3 minutes or until translucent around edges before adding crushed garlic and sautéing until fragrant.

Add red pepper flakes, dried oregano, dried basil and tomato paste, sauce additional minute.

Add crushed tomato, simmer 20-30 minutes, stir regularly to avoid scorching.

Should be able to drag spoon through it and leave a trail ("Moses Parting the Red Sea" joke goes here)

Baking

Preheat oven to 425°F

Re stretch into cast iron to form crust

Remove sausage from casings and add in thin layer, raw, it bakes, you're fine. Break it up as much as possible, we don't want a large suasage patty.

Add layer of sliced provolone followed by a layer of pepperoni then layer of mozzarella.

Pour and spread a layer of sauce.

Bake for 30 minutes, rotate once during baking

Remove from pan and let rest 15 minutes, ideally on a wire rack, to cool before cutting.

Reheating

Preheat oven to 450°F

Bake, tented with foil 20 minutes
