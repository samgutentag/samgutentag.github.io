Babish Deep Dish Pizza

Formatted

Servings: 12" Cast iron skillet, 4 servings Source: Binging with Babish

Ingredients

8.25 oz room temp water

2.25 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast

1 tsp sugar

355 g all purpose Flour

85 g corn meal

1½ tsp kosher salt

⅛ tsp Cream of Tartar

½ cup vegetable oil


28 oz San Marzano Tomatos, Hand Crushed

1 white onion, finely diced

4 garlic clove, crushed

2 Tbsp olive oil

2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes

2 tsp dried Oregano

2 tsp dried Basil

1 6oz can tomato paste


1 pound sausage, removed from casing

1 pkg sliced pepperoni

16 oz provolone, sliced or shredded (sliced preferred)

16 os low moisture mozzarella, sliced or shredded (sliced preferred)

Directions

Pizza Crust

In a Stand Mixer Bowl combine water, dry active yeast and sugar. Allow to bloom for 10 minutes, should be foamy

In another bowl whisk to combine flour, corn meal, kosher salt and cream of tartar

Pour mixture into stand mixer bowl and add olive oil

Stir with dough hook on Low (sipped 2 on Stand Mixer) for 1-2 minutes, until dough comes from bowl

Stir on medium (4-5 on Stand Mixer) another 7-8 minutes to knead

Knead additional 2 minutes by hand on floured surface

Stretch into ball, place into lightly oiled bowl, cover and let rest 60-90 minutes or until doubled in size*

Oil cast iron skillet and stretch dough into crust

Let rest, covered 25 minutes to relax

Pizza Sauce

In large sauce pan add olive oil and heat over medium heat until shimmering.

Add onion and sweat 3 minutes or until translucent around edges before adding crushed garlic and sautéing until fragrant.

Add red pepper flakes, dried oregano, dried basil and tomato paste, sauce additional minute.

Add crushed tomato, simmer 30 minutes, stir regularly to avoid scorching.

Should be able to drag spoon through it and leave a trail ("Moses Parting the Red Sea" joke goes here)

In the cast iron pan you intended to bake in, crumble and brown sausage over medium high heat, save for later. Wipe out excess grease from pan.

Baking

Preheat oven to 425°F

Re stretch into cast iron to form crust

Layer in fillings:

1. Sausage

2. Provolone

3. Pepperoni

4. Mozzarella

5. Sauce

Add cooked sausage in an even layer, press very gently into corners to help form crust edge. DO NOT press in too firmly, crust will separate from bottom if too thin at outer corner

Add layer of sliced provolone followed by a layer of pepperoni then layer of mozzarella.

Pour and spread a layer of sauce.

Bake for 40 minutes, rotate once during baking, do not rotate if using a convecrion oven.

Remove from pan and let rest 15 minutes, ideally on a wire rack, to cool before cutting.

Reheating

Preheat oven to 450°F

Bake, tented with foil 20 minutes
