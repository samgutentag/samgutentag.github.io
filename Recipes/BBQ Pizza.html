BBQ Pizza

Formatted

Servings: 4

Ingredients


1 cup barbecue sauce

1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

1 medium sweet potato, ringed and quarterd

1/2 cup pepperoncinis, banana peppers

1 yellow sweet onion, chopped

1 lb chicken breast




380 g 00 tipo flour (or all purpose)

10 g sugar

6 g fine sea salt

1 g diastatic malt powder

245 g water

5 g instant yeast


1/4 cup all purpose four

1/4 cup corn meal

Directions

Prep Toppings

Preheat oven to 425F.

Cut sweet potato into rings roughly 1/4" thick, then cut rings into quarters or small bit sized pieces.

Toss in oil and bake in single layer for 25 minutes or until fork tender

Chop onion into bite sized pieces.

In a large non stick skillet, pan cook chicken breast, transfer to large bowl and shred.

Toss shredded chicken back into pan and fry again to crisp the pieces.

Spread barbecue sauce evenly over naan bread.

Top sauced naan with sweet potato, chicken and onion, sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese.

Pizza Dough
Pizza Dough

Combine flour, sugar, sea salt and diastatic malt powder to large bowl, whisk to combine thoroughly.

Warm water to about 100 degrees F and add yeast, stir and let rest 5 minutes to bloom and bubble.

Pour liquid over flour mixture and mix by hand until a shaggy dough forms.

Transfer to lightly floured work surface and knead to a soft velvety surface. Don't worry if its too sticky at first, last resort add a smidge more flour.

Shape into a tight ball on and place in a lightly oiled bowl.

Cover bowl tightly in plastic wrap and let rise at room temperature 1-2 hours to until doubled in size OR in the refrigerator overnight.

Combine equal parts flour and corn meal for second kneading work, lightly dust your work surface and a generously cover a sheet pan

Divide dough in half and shape into medium taught balls again and let rest in a sheet tray, covered, at least 30 minutes. this step is important!

Generously dust work surface and dough with corn meal flour mixture while working

Generously cover pizza peal with corn meal flour mixture before placing dough and topping

Bake on pizza steel preheated to 500Â°F for 8-9 minutes.

Notes

If making with Naan instead of pizza dough, cook on a baking sheet at 450F for 3-4 minutes, remove and add toppings, then bake an additional 3-4 minutes or until the cheese has melted
