Overnight Oats

Prep Time: 10 min Total Time: 10 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 5

Ingredients

5 cups oat milk

5 cups gluten free quick oats

2 cups frozen blueberries

2 cups frozen mango chunks

3 bananas, sliced into rings

Directions

Layer scoop of oats and berries in a mason jar as follows:

⅓ Cup oats
½ Sliced banana
⅓ Cup oats
½ Cup berries or diced mango. Shake to settle
⅓ Cup oats

Pour in 1 cup oat milk and allow to settle until fully saturated

Secure lid and shake to combine.

Store in fridge until ready to eat, at rest 4 hours.

Notes

Use a chopstick or spoon to help liquid move down past bubbles into the depths of the jar.

Often made Sunday mid day and last through Friday morning breakfast
