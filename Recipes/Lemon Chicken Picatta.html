Lemon Chicken Picatta

Formatted

Servings: 4

Ingredients

3 tsp capers

6 Tbsp butter

3 garlic cloves, crushed

3 Tbsp lemon Juice (1 lemon per two servings)

1 1/2 lb chicken breast, sliced thin lengthwise

2 cups flour

2 Tbsp olive oil

1 box pasta of choice

Directions

In a small sauce pan, melt butter and add crushed garlic, zest of 1 lemon, and capers. Simmer over low heat until ready to serve.

Slice chicken breast as thin as able, or place in a ziplock back and bound flat, 1/4 inch thickness is ideal.

Pat dry with paper towels, DONT FORGET THIS STEP

Combine flower, salt, pepper and pour onto a plate or shallow bowl. Cover chicken breast in mixture completely.

Heat olive oil over medium high heat in al large skillet and cook chicken until golden. For crispier skin, combine additional 1 Tbsp melted butter to olive oil before cooking chicken.

Cook pasta according to box instructions.
