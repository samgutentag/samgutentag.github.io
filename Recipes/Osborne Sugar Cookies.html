Osborne Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

3 cup powdered Sugar

2 cups butter

2 eggs

2 tsp vanilla

1 tsp almond

5 cup flour, sifted

2 tsp baking soda

2 tsp cream of tartar

Directions

Cream wet ingredients butter eggs vanilla

Separate dry ingredients

Refrigerate for two hours or longer in parchment paper

Powder sugar to roll out thick on counter

Bake on use parchment paper at 350 for 8.5 minutes
