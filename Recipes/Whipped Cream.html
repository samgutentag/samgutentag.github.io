Whipped Cream

Formatted

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Source: Freshaprilflours.com

Ingredients

1 cup heavy cream or heavy whipping cream

1 Tbsp sugar

1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

Place mixing bowl and whisk attachment in freezer for 5-10 minutes prior to starting.

Pour the heavy whipping cream, sugar, and vanilla extract into the bowl.

Turn the mixer to medium speed (4 on stand mixer).

After about 2 to 3 minutes, the mixture will first get frothy and bubbly, then start to thicken

Once it is visibly thick (you will be able to see the trail of the whisk), increase the speed to medium-high (6 on stand mixer) and beat for an additional 30 seconds to 1 minute.

Use immediately on your favorite dessert. Store whipped cream covered tightly in the refrigerator up to 2 days.
