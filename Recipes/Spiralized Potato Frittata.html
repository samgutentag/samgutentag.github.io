Spiralized Potato Frittata

Formatted

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 30 Total Time: 40 Difficulty: Medium Servings: 4 Source: Inspiralized.com

Ingredients

1/2 lb bacon

3 small Yukon Gold potatoes

1/4 tsp garlic powder

salt and pepper

12 eggs

1 Tbsp chopped chives

3 mixed bell peppers

1 bunch green onion, diced

4 garlic cloves, crushed

3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.

Spiralize potatoes with large flat blade or medium noodle blade, set aside. For more manageable pieces, cut stack of rings into half moons.

Cut bacon into ½“ peices and cook over medium high heat in a 12” cast iron skillet until crispy. Transfer to a paper towel lined plate.

Immediately add the potato and season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.

Toss in the bacon grease and let cook, tossing occasionally until cooked through, about 7 minutes.

Add crushed garlic and toss to mix in.

Whisk the eggs together in a medium bowl and add the chives, peppers and cheddar cheese.

Once the potatoes are done cooking, spread them out evenly on the bottom of the skillet.

Add bacon back to the skillet, in an even layer as possible.

Pour over the egg mixture and swirl the pan until eggs cover the potatoes. Cook until eggs are set on the bottom, 2-3 minutes.

Transfer the skillet into the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes or until eggs are completely set. You will see bubbling on the sides from the bacon grease.

Remove from the oven and slice into four or eight slices. Serve immediately topped with green onion.

Notes

This is scaled for a 12" skillet
