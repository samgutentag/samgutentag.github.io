Instant Pot Cilantro Lime Rice

Prep Time: 4 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: 4 Source: kitchenathoskins.com

Ingredients

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1/4 cup chopped onion

2 garlic cloves, chopped

1 cup basmati rice, washed, rinsed and drained

1 and 1/2 cups chicken stock

1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon salt, refer notes

zest of one lime

juice of 1 and 1/2 limes

1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Directions

Set Instant pot to saute setting. Once hot, add vegetable oil and then onion and garlic. Saute for about 2 minutes, until garlic begins to turn golden.

Add rice and continue to saute for about 30 seconds. Stir in stock, salt and lime zest. Close, lock lid and make sure the valve is set to sealing. Select rice setting and let Instant pot do its thing (it took 12 minutes to cook in mine).

When instant pot beeps, let continue to sit for 10 minutes. Click cancel button and then turn valve to vent and let pressure release. Open lid and stir in lime juice and cilantro.

Serve hot or warm with your favorite Mexican meal.
