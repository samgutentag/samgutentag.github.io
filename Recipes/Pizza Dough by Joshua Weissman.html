Pizza Dough by Joshua Weissman

Formatted

Servings: 2 10 inch pizza doughs Source: Joshua Weissman

Ingredients

380 g 00 tipo flour (or all purpose)

10 g sugar

6 g fine sea salt

1 g diastatic malt powder

245 g water

5 g instant yeast


1/4 cup all purpose four

1/4 cup corn meal

Directions

Combine flour, sugar, sea salt and diastatic malt powder to large bowl, whisk to combine thuroughly.

Warm water to about 100 degrees F and add yeast, stir and let rest 5 minutes to bloom and bubble.

Pour liquid over flour mixture and mix by hand until a shaggy dough forms.

Transfer to lightly floured work surface and knead to a soft velvety surface. Don't worry if its too sticky at first, last resort add a smidge more flour.

Shape into a tight ball on and place in a lightly oiled bowl.

Pro tip: lightly brush plastic wrap with olive oil to prevent sticking.

Cover bowl tightly in plastic wrap and let rise at room temperature 1-2 hours to until doubled in size OR in the refrigerator overnight.

Combine equal parts flour and corn meal for second kneading work, lightly dust your work surface and a generously cover a sheet pan

Divide dough in half and shape into medium taught balls again and let rest in a sheet tray, covered, at least 30 minutes.

Generously dust work surface and dough with corn meal flour mixture while working

Generously cover pizza peal with corn meal flour mixture before placing dough and topping


Bake on pizza steel pregeated to 500Â°F for 7-9 minutes
