Red Chicken Curry

Formatted

Servings: 4 Source: keviniscooking.com

Ingredients

1 lb chicken thigh or breast meat, sliced thin

1/4 cup vegetable oil

2/3 cup Thai Red Curry Paste

1/4 cup fish sauce

1/4 cup peanut butter

1 Tbsp sugar

2 Limes

15oz coconut milk

1 red fresno chile or jalapeño sliced

3 mixed bell pepper sliced thin strips

1 large potato peeled, cubed and cooked

Directions

Peal and Cube potatoes, roughly 1/4 inch cubes. Place in largest and fill with cold water.

Bring water to boil from cold, and allow to boil 3-5 minutes or until tender. Start next steps when you start the burner.

In a wok or large skillet over medium heat add the oil and curry paste. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring often.

Add the chicken and cook for another 3 minutes.

Add the fish sauce, peanut butter, sugar, ½ lime juice , coconut milk and stir. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2 minutes.

Add sliced bell peppers, cooked potato cubes and cook another 3 minutes until chicken is cooked through. Sauce should thicken and reduce.

Serve with steamed rice and sliced limes to squeeze on top.
