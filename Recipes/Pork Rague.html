Pork Rague

Cook Time: 20 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 3 Source: countryliving.com

Ingredients

1 Tbsp. olive oil

1 lb. ground pork

2 cloves garlic, pressed

3 oz tomato paste

1/2 cup dry white wine (Nobilo)

1/2 cup chicken stock/broth

1/4 cup fresh flat leaf parsley, chopped

2 Tbsp. fresh chives, chopped

1 Tbsp. fresh tarragon, chopped

1/2 cup grated Parmesan, for serving

Salt and pepper

1 pkg gnocchi

Directions

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat

Add pressed garlic and cook, stir until fragrant, about 1 minute

Add Ground Pork and season with Salt and Pepper

Cook, breaking up into small pieces until browned, about 4-6 minutes, drain off excess fat as needed

Meanwhile, place a large pot over high heat to bring water to boil for gnocchi, once boiling, cook according to package instructions.

Add Tomato Paste to pork and continue cooking until it starts to get crispy, about 3 minutes

Add White Wine and simmer until mostly evaporated, about 3 minutes

Scrape bottom of pan to assist with deglazing if needed

Add Chicken Stock/Broth and bring to a simmer, remove from heat

Stir in Parsley, Chives, and Tarragon
