BBQ Naan Pizza

Servings: 4

Ingredients

4 naan bread pieces

1 cup barbecue sauce

1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded/sliced

1 medium sweet potato, ringed and quartered

1 yellow sweet onion, chopped

1 lb chicken breast

Directions

Preheat oven to 425F.

Cut sweet potato into rings roughly 1/4" thick, then cut rings into quarters or small bit sized pieces.

Toss in oil and bake in single layer for 25 minutes or until fork tender

Chop onion into bite sized pieces.

In a large non stick skillet, pan cook chicken breast, transfer to large bowl and shred.

Toss shredded chicken back into pan and fry again to crisp the pieces.

Spread barbecue sauce evenly over naan bread.

Top sauced naan with sweet potato, chicken and onion, sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese.

Bake on the oven rack for 8 minutes or until cheese has melted.

Notes

For crunchier naan bread bake the naan for 3-4 minutes in the oven before adding toppings, carefully remove -- add sauce, cheese, extra toppings, and bake for 3-4 more minutes or until cheese has melted.
