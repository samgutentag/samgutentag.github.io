Fresh Pasta Dough by Babish

Servings: About 1 pound Source: Babish

Ingredients

300g All Purpose Flour

5g Kosher Salt

50g Egg (about 1 Large)

80g Water

10g Olive Oil

Directions

Sift flour and kosher salt together into the bowl of a stand mixer, stir to combine.

Add in eggs, water, and olive oil to bowl.

Mix first on 'Stir' then on 2 or 3 until a dough ball forms.

Dough should be well incorporated but dry and will break into chunks in your hands.

Press into disk and wrap tightly in plastic wrap. Allow to rest at room temp for 30 minutes.

Generously dust a cookie sheet or plate to collect extruded noodles.

Attach Pasta Extruder and Bucatini Plate, on highest speed feed walnut sized balls of dough into hopper.

Trim at roughly 1 inch pieces, dust generously in flour, don't let them stack.

Toss in a sieve noodles gently to remove excess flour before cooking.

Pasta should cook in roughly 90 seconds in boiling water. Let water return to boil between batches!

Notes

Dough can be made 1 day ahead; tightly wrap and chill.
