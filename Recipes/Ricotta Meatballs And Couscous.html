Ricotta Meatballs And Couscous

Formatted

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 60 mins Servings: 6 Source: girlgonemom.com

Ingredients

1 lb ground pork

1 lb ground beef

1 cup whole milk ricotta

2 eggs

⅓ cup italian breadcrumbs

2 garlic cloves, pressed

¼ cup dried parsley

1½ tsp kosher salt

½ tsp pepper

3 boxes couscous

2 28oz jars Arrabbiata Sauce

Directions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

Mix all of the ingredients together in a large mixing bowl with clean hands.

Shape meatballs by rolling between palms.

Meatballs should be rough 50g each, slightly larger than a golf ball. Base recipe yields 24 meatballs at this size.

Bake meatballs on an edged cookie sheet or jelly roll pan for 40 minutes.

Place meatballs in a large pot of sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
