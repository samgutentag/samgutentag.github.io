Savory Cornmeal Waffles

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 8 Source: loveandoliveoil.com

Ingredients

345g all-purpose flour

115g stone ground yellow cornmeal

2 tsp baking soda

1 tsp baking powder

3/4 tsp kosher salt

1/4 tsp ground cayenne

12 oz whole milk

1 large lemon, juiced

2 large eggs, separated

1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled to lukewarm

1 Tbsp honey

Directions

Combine milk and lemon juice, let stand while prepping other ingredients. Milk will slightly curdle.

Preheat Oven to 225ºF and plug in Square waffle iron.

In a large bowl, sift together flour and cornmeal

Add baking soda, baking powder, salt, and cayenne, whisk to combine.

In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg yolks, melted butter, and honey until just incorporated. Do not overmix.

Pour wet ingredients into flour mixture, combine with a spatula with a turning motion, do not over mix the batter.

In a medium bowl, whisk egg whites until they hold semi-stiff peaks. Fold whites into batter.

Ladle a scant cup of batter into the waffle iron, spread gently with spatula.

Close the lid and pin down the lid with the handle as the batter heats up and fills upwards in the waffle iron, about first 30 seconds.

Cook for about 4 minutes or until waffles are deep golden brown and crisp.

Transfer to a baking sheet in a 225ºF oven to keep warm.

Repeat with remaining batter.


Chili Cheese Waffles:

Mix 1 cup of shredded cheese into batter prior to cooking. We like a mix of 1/2 cup white cheddar, 1/4 cup manchego, and 1/4 cup grated parmesan. Top with your favorite hearty chili, sour cream, shredded cheese, and fresh cilantro.

Sunnyside-Up Herb Waffles:

Mix 2/3 cup of chopped fresh herbs such as basil, parsley, and chives into waffle batter. Top with a fried or poached egg and drizzle with creamy hollandaise sauce.

Waffles & Greens:

Toss fresh arugula, watercress, or other spicy green with a squeeze of lemon juice, a splash of olive oil, and a dash of salt and pepper. Top each waffle with a handful of greens, crumbled goat cheese, and roasted pistachios.
