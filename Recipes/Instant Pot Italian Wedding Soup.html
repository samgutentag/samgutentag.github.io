Instant Pot Italian Wedding Soup

Prep Time: 10 m Cook Time: 35 m Servings: 4 servings Source: Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 tablespoon olive oil

2½ cup carrots, chopped

1½ cup celery, sliced into rings

1 red onion, diced

2 teaspoon dried parsley

¾ teaspoon dried basil

¾ teaspoon ground black pepper

9 cups no-salt-added chicken broth

30 Italian meatballs, Chef John's Italian Meatballs

1½ cup orzo pasta

10 ounces baby spinach

8 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese

salt to taste

Directions

Set Instant Pot to Saute, heat oil until just shimmering and add carrots, celery and red onion; cook until tender, 4 to 5 minutes.

Season with parsley, basil and pepper, to taste.

Add Broth and Meatballs to pot; close and seal lid.

Set Instant Pot to Soup and set timer for 3 minutes. Allow pressure to build fully.

After 3 minutes, Quick release pressure and remove lid, stir in pasting; put lid back on.

Set Instant Pot to Soup and set timer for 3 minutes. Allow pressure to build fully.

After 3 minutes, Quick release pressure and remove lid, stir in spinach and let sit for 5 minutes. so pasta will be fully cooked and spinach will be wilted.

Season with salt to taste.

Ladle into bowls and top with Parmesan cheese.
