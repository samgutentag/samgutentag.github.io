Lemonade

Formatted

Prep Time: 10 minutes Total Time: 10 min Servings: Serves 6 Source: simplyrecipes.com

Ingredients

1½ cup white, granulated sugar

3 cup water (for the simple syrup)

3 cup lemon juice

9 cups cold water (to dilute)

Directions

Combine ¼ of water and sugar in a sauce pan over medium heat, stir mixture until sugar is dissolved

Add lemon juice to serving pitcher and half of remaining cold water.

Pour in simple syrup and remaining cold water, stir to combine.

Refrigerate 30 to 40 minutes.

Serve with ice, sliced lemons.
