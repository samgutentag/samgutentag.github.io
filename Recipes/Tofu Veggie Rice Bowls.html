Tofu Veggie Rice Bowls

Servings: 2

Ingredients

1 block plain Hodo Tofu

1 large head broccoli

2 large carrots

1 cup rice

1 cup water

4 Tbsp Soy Vay Teriyaki sauce

Directions

Start Instant Pot Rice.

Cut tofu block once hanger style, then hotdog style, then into 1/2 wide strips, ideally making tofu pillars, square and half the width of the tofu block.

Cut carrots on an angle into roughly 1/4 inch rings

In a large skillet over medium high heat add 1 Tbsp olive oil and add carrots, add 2 Tbsp water and cover to steam, about 4 minutes.

Trim crowns from broccoli, add to skillet with carrots and cover, steaming an additional 2 minutes. Add more water if necessary.

Once steamed, remove cover to prevent veggies getting soggy.

In a second skillet, add tofu pillars in a single layer, allow to cook over medium high heat until just starting to brown, about 3 minutes.

Rotate or toss tofu until all sides are crisped slightly, then add Teriyaki Sauce and toss to combine.

Let cook additional 2-3 minutes.

Top Rice with veggies and tofu, serve with Sriricha sauce while hot!
