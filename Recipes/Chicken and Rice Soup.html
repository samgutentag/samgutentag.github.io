Chicken and Rice Soup

Formatted

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: 6 Source: dinneratthezoo.com

Ingredients

2 Tbsp butter

1 onion, finely diced

3 carrots, peeled, halved lengthwise, and sliced

1 cup celery sliced

1 Tbsp garlic, minced

1 3/4 lb chicken breast, shredded

2 tsp Italian seasoning

salt and pepper to taste

9 cups chicken broth

3 cups white rice

3 Tbsp chopped parsley

Directions

Prepare chicken, slice thin and cook in skillet on the stove. Shread and set aside.

Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat.

Add the onion, carrots and celery to the pot and cook for 4-5 minutes or until softened.

Add the garlic to the pot and cook for 30 seconds.

Add the chicken, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer.

Cook for 10-15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.

Stir in the rice.

Cook for 5 minutes.

Sprinkle with parsley, then serve.
