Cream Scones

Servings: 8 Source: Joy of Cooking, page 637

Ingredients

2½ cups (315 g) all purpose flour

2¼ tsp baking powder

¼ cup (50 g) sugar

½ tsp salt

1¼ cups (290 g) heavy cream

1 tsp vanilla

Turbinado Sugar (optional)

Directions

Preheat oven to 450°F

Whisk together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in large bowl

Stir together heavy cream and vanilla

Make a well in the flour mixture

Pour the cream mixture and combine in a few swift strokes

Handle the dough as little as possible

Turn it out onto a lightly floured work surface.

Pat into a flat 8-inch round disk and cut woth a floured bench scraper into 8-12 wedges

Alternatively pat flat and use a floured pastry cutter or biscuit punch

Brush with heavy cream, sprinkle with turbinado sugar if desired

Place on and insulated baking sheet or use two baking sheets and bake until golden brown, about 19 minutes

Serve warm or at room temperature
